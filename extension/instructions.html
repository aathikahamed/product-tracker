Find similar products:

Search any products you want, this extension will find the best rated and the lowest priced product available on the internet along with some other options.

  • ★You will get the title, price, rating and the image of the product.
  • ★You can click the title of the product to take you to the website that has listed the product.

Reduced price notifier:

This involves a little work but once the setup is complete it will continuously look for price reduction and will notify you instantly.

  • ★ Inspect element
  • ★Hover over the price to get the Id
  • ★Enter the id and your email address

Stock availability notifier:

  • ★ Enter a keyword that might change when stock becomes available, for example: Unavailable which will change to Available, "Out of Stock" will change to Stock available and so on. As soon as a change it detected, this extension will notify you instantly.

Amazon exclusive price history:

This feature is currently available for Amazon only

  • ★When you are on a product page of Amazon's website, open the extension you will find the price history of the product instantly.
